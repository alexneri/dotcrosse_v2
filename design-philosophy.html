Design Philosophy

Simple. Elegant. Modern.

Every work I've done all have but the same precepts used in its creation: the tenets of Simplicity, Elegance and Progressive. Resuting from this is a work that is straightforward, deliberate, succinct, and concise.

Simplicity

At the core of every design is the idea of simplicity. It is the deep understanding of what really matters, and of what should be. Each design has a focus in which everything else defers to.

The right things at the right time. Nothing extra. Only there when you need it.

And that is the tenet of Simplicity.

Elegance

Elegance is all about grace. The more forceful something feels, the lesser it is for people to identify with the subject. On the other hand, leaving things without impact leaves a lesser impression to the user.

Animations, angles, colours, lines, and whitespaces. All these should work in concert to deliver gentle, impactful, and an impressive presentation to leave the user a delightful experience.

Because Elegance, above all, is all about User Experience.

Progressive

Sometimes, designing for the needs of the user is not enough.
