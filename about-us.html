About DotCrosse

Quality Engineer | UX/Web Designer | Creative

Hello, my name is Alex Neri. A Software Quality Engineer who has a penchant for anything design, UX, creativity, and Japanimation. Feel free to have a look around, and be sure to drop by my Twitter @DotCrosse.

Most of my work are usually art, design, and audio-related. I also write stories, with a list which can be found in its own section in the site. Articles can be found with their respective publications, unless specified. For QA, a complete histpry of projects I've worked on can be found here.

As you may have noticed, the handle DotCrosse has been my identity primarily with Twitter. However, with a growing number of people who knew me by that name, I've started to make use of it more commonly than my other identities (My other names, like "Crosse" or "Sairene" are quite short have been quickly taken by others).

Again, thank you for stopping by!
