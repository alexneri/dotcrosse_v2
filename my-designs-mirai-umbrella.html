MIRAI UMBRELLA

Umbrella, Product Design

Mirai Suenaga is the trademark mascot of the site DannyChoo.com, and this project was done as part of a design pool he was having.

In building this design, I adjusted the original colour palettes to mute them a bit, to give more breathing room to the eyes when looking at the design. Used various brushes, most of which I have the license to use. Ah, there's a brush that only needed a linkback when used. You can find the Brush here.
